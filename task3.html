Blog Name:Cozzy Kitchen

INGRIDENTS:

  1. 3/4 cup melted coconut oil
  2. 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  3. 2-4 tablespoons naturak sweetner
  4. pinch of salt
  5. 3/4 cup almond butter
  6. choco chips for topping
INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Whisk the coconut oil,cocoa powder,sweetner,and add a pinch of salt.
  • Fill a regular size muffin tin with paper liners .
  • Pour a small amount of thecocoa mixture into the paper cups.Drop a small amount of the almond butter into the center of each cup
  • If almond butter is sticking up,just gently press it down so each cup has a smooth top layer.
  • Sprinkle each almond butter cup with a choco chips.Freeze fo 1 hour or until solid
